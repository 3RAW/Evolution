Evolution Fun Facts

Read on and get amazed!

  • Blond hair in humans developed only 11,000 years ago!
  • The rhyme "she sells sea shells on the sea shore" is based on Mary Anning, the famous fossil hunter.
  • The earliest biological evolution started about 3.7 billion years ago.
  • We share 70% of our DNA with a slug!
  • Birds evolved from reptiles
  • Many people think that dinosaurs were the first reptile to evolve, but they only arrived 220 million years ago. The first reptile to actually evolve was 340 million years ago!
  • Human faces evolved to take a punch better
  • You may all think that pandas eat bamboo but did you know that they used be carnivores?
  • Penguin wings evolved to flippers
  • Evolution is causing the human brain to shrink!
